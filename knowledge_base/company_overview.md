# Purple Innovation, Inc. (NASDAQ: PRPL) — Company Overview

> Source: Purple Innovation FY2025 results (PR Newswire / investors.purple.com,
> reported March 2026) and public filings. All figures are real and public.

## Business
Purple Innovation is a U.S. digitally-native vertical brand (DTVB) that designs
and manufactures premium comfort products — mattresses, pillows, cushions, and
bedding. Its core differentiation is proprietary, patented gel technology, the
**GelFlex Grid**, built on 30+ years of comfort-technology innovation. Purple
both manufactures its products and sells them.

## Channels
- **Direct-to-consumer (DTC):** e-commerce (purple.com) and Purple-owned
  showrooms.
- **Wholesale:** third-party retail partners — notably an expanded
  **Mattress Firm** partnership and **Costco** programs.
- In 2025, e-commerce declined while wholesale grew, shifting channel mix toward
  wholesale.

## Sector
Premium sleep / mattress and home-comfort products — a niche, brand- and
technology-led segment of the broader bedding industry.

## Strategic posture (2025–2026)
- Turnaround focus: disciplined cost actions, manufacturing consolidation,
  sourcing savings, and reinforcing premium positioning.
- The **Rejuvenate 2.0** mattress line outperformed expectations.
- The Board initiated a **review of strategic alternatives** in March 2025
  (potential sale, merger, or other transaction) after inbound interest; the
  review remained ongoing into 2026.
