# Purple Innovation — Competitive Landscape & Risks

> Source: Purple public filings (10-K), PR Newswire, Retail Dive, StockTitan,
> CB Insights (2025–2026). Real, public information.

## Direct competitors (mattress / sleep)
- **Tempur Sealy International (NYSE: TPX)** — largest bedding company; mass and
  premium scale, broad wholesale distribution.
- **Sleep Number (NASDAQ: SNBR)** — smart/adjustable beds, DTC + retail.
- **Casper** (private) — DTC mattress pioneer.
- **Helix Sleep** — personalization-focused online mattresses.
- **Haverty Furniture (NYSE: HVT)** — furniture retailer carrying bedding.

## Purple's differentiation
Proprietary, patented **GelFlex Grid** gel technology (30+ years of comfort R&D)
distinguishes Purple's feel and pressure relief from foam/innerspring rivals.

## Material risks (real, disclosed)
- **Going concern:** auditor going-concern emphasis tied to negative operating
  cash flow (~$33.8M in 2025) and limited cash ($24.3M). In March 2026 lenders
  waived the requirement that FY2025 statements be delivered without a going-
  concern qualification.
- **Debt / control concentration:** total borrowings $111.3M. **Coliseum
  Capital** is Purple's largest stockholder and lender (holds ~$81.8M of debt
  and ~61.1M shares), giving it substantial control over board, covenants, and
  any capital solution. In March 2026 the credit-agreement maturity was extended
  to April 30, 2027.
- **Channel shift:** e-commerce declining while wholesale (Mattress Firm,
  Costco) grows — wholesale typically carries lower margin and more partner
  dependence.
- **Strategic uncertainty:** Board's review of strategic alternatives (sale /
  merger) ongoing since March 2025.
