# Purple Innovation — Financials (FY2025) and 2026 Guidance

> Source: "Purple Innovation Reports Fourth Quarter and Full Year 2025 Results"
> (PR Newswire / investors.purple.com, March 2026). Real, public figures.

## Full Year 2025
- **Net revenue: $468.7M** (down 3.9% YoY) — decline driven by e-commerce
  headwinds, partly offset by wholesale growth.
- **Gross profit: $188.6M; gross margin 40.2%** (up 310 bps YoY) — reflects
  restructuring, sourcing savings, and manufacturing efficiencies.
- **Net loss: $(51.4)M** (improved from $(97.9)M in 2024).
- **Adjusted EBITDA: $1.9M** (turned positive from $(20.8)M in 2024).

## Q4 2025
- Net revenue: $140.7M (up ~9.1% YoY).
- Gross margin: 41.9%; net loss $(3.2)M; adjusted EBITDA $8.8M.

## Balance sheet / liquidity (year-end 2025)
- **Cash: $24.3M** (down from $29.0M).
- Inventories: $59.7M.
- **Total borrowings / related-party debt: $111.3M** (up from $55.4M).
- Negative operating cash flow of ~$33.8M in 2025.

## 2026 Guidance
- Full-year revenue: **$500–$520M**.
- Full-year adjusted EBITDA: **$20–$30M**.
- Q1 2026 revenue: $100–$105M; Q1 2026 adjusted EBITDA: $(7)–$(4)M.

## Unit-economics context
- The business is volume/brand driven (premium mattresses), not subscription —
  revenue is product sales across DTC and wholesale, with gross margin ~40%.
